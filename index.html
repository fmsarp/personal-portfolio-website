Cryptocurrencies

2017 - Present

Heard about Bitcoin in 2015 from a friend who worked for an internet cafe back in Ghana. I wasn't able to acquire some BTC because it was not easy doing it from where I was and had no spare money or source of income to supplement this activity. In 2017 however, this would slightly change after I was trying to find hoe make money online and came across a website called "freebitcoin" (I will leave referral link below). This website is a bitcoin faucet that gives you free satoshies when you visit it every hour and click on the "roll" button. I didn't know much about the working mechanism behind crypto at that time so I lost much of the sats I acquired. Fast forward to today and I still use that website which I have a Youtube video about it on my channel in two languagues. I hold onto my sats dearly now since I took time when I was out of work in 2021 to learn more about what crypto really is and how it works. I even had my own bitcoin node setup in my apartment and hope to be running a node infrastructure company.
